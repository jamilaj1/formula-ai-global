The Founder

A journey from the lab to artificial intelligence

With 25+ years of hands-on chemical manufacturing across multiple countries — currently overseeing operations producing over 2,000 tons per month, and founder of his own plant DosLunas (50+ tons/month) — Jamil saw a challenge facing every chemist around the world:

  • × Outdated chemistry references full of obsolete ingredient names
  • × Global databases locked behind expensive subscriptions
  • × Country-specific regulations with no unified tool
