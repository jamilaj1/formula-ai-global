How the simulator works

15+ properties predicted from first principles

pH calculation

Henderson-Hasselbalch with ingredient pKa values + buffer capacity adjustment.

Viscosity

Concentration × interaction matrix (SLES + salt curve, etc.) with Arrhenius temperature correction.

Surface tension

Szyszkowski equation for surfactants above CMC, weighted average across ingredients.

Stability score

Penalty stack: anionic+cationic, pH extremes, oil-water without emulsifier, microbial risk.

Shelf life

Base 730 days × pH penalty × stability multiplier × ingredient sensitivity (peroxide capped at 180d).

Process recommendations

Optimal mixing temperature (45°C if surfactant), RPM and time tuned to viscosity range.
